## Key Tips:

✅ **For Students:** Use "Student" in Profession field and your college name in Company/Institution field
✅ **For Professionals:** List your job title and company
✅ **For Business Owners:** List what business you're in and company names
✅ **Bio:** Use this to briefly describe your journey and expertise
✅ **How I Can Help:** List what guidance/help you can offer to other Navodayans
✅ **Social Links:** At least one social link makes you more reachable!
